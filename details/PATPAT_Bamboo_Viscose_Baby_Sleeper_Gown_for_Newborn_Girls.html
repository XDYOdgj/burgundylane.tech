Soft Bamboo Viscose Baby Sleeper Gown for Newborn Girls

This adorable baby sleeper gown is crafted from ultra-soft bamboo viscose fabric, combining 93% bamboo with 10% spandex for unmatched comfort and breathability. Perfect for your newborn girl, it keeps her skin cool, dry, and free from irritation throughout the night.

The gentle, hypoallergenic formula of the fabric ensures it's safe for sensitive skin, giving parents peace of mind. With a stylish, embroidered headband and fold-over mittens, this gown offers both charm and protection from accidental scratches. It's ideal for sleep, and helps keep little hands warm during bedtime.

The open-bottom design makes nighttime diaper changes a breeze, while the adjustable knot provides a secure, customizable fit that grows with your baby. Whether you're looking for a hospital homecoming outfit, a photoshoot essential, or a heartfelt gift for new parents, this sleeper gown is both functional and fashionable.

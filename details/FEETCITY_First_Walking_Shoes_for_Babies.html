FEETCITY First Walking Shoes

Designed to support your baby's early steps, these First Walking Shoes are built for comfort, durability, and style. With a breathable knitted mesh upper, they keep your little one's feet cool and dry, helping to prevent odor and ensuring all-day wearability.
